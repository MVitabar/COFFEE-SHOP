Our Coffees

Variety and Quality in Every Cup

Americano: A coffee prepared by adding hot water to espresso, resulting in a lighter flavor and concentration compared to pure espresso.

Latte: An espresso softened with steamed milk and topped with a layer of milk foam. It is a creamy and smooth beverage, popular for breakfast.

Cappuccino: Made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. Often served with cocoa or cinnamon sprinkled on top.

Espresso: A strong and concentrated coffee, served in small quantities, known for its crema and intense flavor.

Macchiato: An espresso "stained" with a small amount of foamed milk on top, giving it a smoother taste than pure espresso.

Mocha: A latte with added chocolate and often topped with whipped cream. It's a favorite choice for chocolate lovers.

Flat White: Similar to a latte but with a higher coffee ratio and a smoother, finer milk texture without the thick foam of a cappuccino.

Iced Coffee: Espresso or Americano served over ice, refreshing and perfect for hot days.

Cortado: An espresso with a touch of warm milk, typical in Spain, which smooths the intensity of the espresso.

Ristretto: A shorter and more concentrated version of espresso, with less water for a richer and less bitter taste.
